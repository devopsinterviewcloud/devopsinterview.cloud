## Notes
- The backend is safer than the storefront: `checkout/route.ts` blocks any non-mapped title with HTTP 409, and `storage.ts` returns `[]` for unwritten books so a paid download is impossible. The exposure is on the marketing surface — advertising, star ratings, testimonials, "Buy Now" on undeliverable SKUs, and a bundle that takes payment while delivering 2 of 5 books (#7).
- `STATUS.md` already documents most of these (fabricated reviews/15k, 3 unwritten books, always-on Coming Soon banner, GST gap) and states the checkout button is "intentionally disabled" — but the live `page.tsx`/`[slug]/page.tsx` Buy Now buttons are fully active, so the doc is stale relative to code.
